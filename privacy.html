Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

1. Who We Are

Echelon is an AI operations consultancy. We build agentic systems and automation workflows for businesses. References to "Echelon," "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to the Echelon team and its operations. For privacy inquiries, contact us at Laurenocampo.biz@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly — name, email address, company name, and project details submitted via booking forms, email, or onboarding questionnaires.
  • Payment information — processed by Paddle, our payment provider. Echelon does not store credit card or banking details. See Paddle's privacy policy for how payment data is handled.
  • Usage data — basic analytics including page visits, referral sources, and browser type when you visit our website. We do not use invasive tracking or sell this data.
  • Client project data — business data, workflows, and operational information shared with us in the course of delivering services. This data is used solely to provide agreed services.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and improve our services
  • To communicate about your engagement (project updates, invoices, scheduling)
  • To send service-related emails — we do not send unsolicited marketing without your consent
  • To meet legal and compliance obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with:

  • Paddle — payment processing. Paddle acts as the Merchant of Record and handles billing on our behalf.
  • Service providers — tools we use to deliver work (e.g., cloud hosting, AI APIs). These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain client data for the duration of the engagement and for up to two (2) years after engagement completion for legal and business continuity purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email Laurenocampo.biz@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies — only what is necessary for basic site functionality and anonymous analytics. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use our site.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no system is completely secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their policies before sharing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our practices evolve. Material changes will be communicated to active clients via email. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at Laurenocampo.biz@gmail.com.
